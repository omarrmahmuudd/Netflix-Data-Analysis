## 📖 Project Overview
This project dives deep into the Netflix dataset to uncover the strategic shifts in content production and distribution. The goal is to provide a data-driven perspective on how Netflix's library has evolved and identify key growth areas.

## 🚀 Strategic Questions Answered
- **Content Mix:** How does Netflix balance between Movies and TV Shows?
- **Global Footprint:** Which countries are the powerhouse of Netflix originals?
- **Genre Evolution:** What are the most trending genres in recent years?
- **Target Audience:** Analysis of content ratings and suitability.

## 🛠️ Data Engineering & Transformation
- **Data Cleaning:** Handled missing values in 'Director' and 'Cast' using Power Query.
- **Feature Engineering:** Extracted 'Month' and 'Year' from 'Date Added' for time-series analysis.
- **DAX Calculations:** Created custom measures for Total Titles, YoY Growth, and Category Distribution.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- **Strategic Shift:** Noticed a significant increase in TV Shows production since 2015 to boost subscriber retention.
- **Market Leaders:** The US and India lead in volume, with a massive surge in South Korean content.
- **Top Genres:** International Movies and Documentaries are the fastest-growing segments.
